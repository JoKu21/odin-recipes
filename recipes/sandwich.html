New York Club Sandwich


This recipe is perfect, if you come home after a long day
and want to enjoy a tasty New York Club Sandwich without spending hours of hours
in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 12 Slices of chicken breast fillet cold cuts (less for large slices)
  • 12 slices of breakfast bacon
  • 4 Eggs (hard boiled)
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 8 Leaves of green salad
  • 100g mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbsp. mustard
  • 12 slices of toast
  • salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Fry the onions until hot and add the minced meat and garlic. Let everything fry for about 5 minutes.
    Deglaze with the pizza tomatoes and the strained tomatoes.
    Season with herbs, sugar, pepper, salt and a little chili - if you like.
    For consistency, add the tomato paste, a whole tube if needed.

  2. Now fill the minced meat mixture thinly into a preferably square baking dish, so that the lasagne plates fit.
    Put a layer of lasagna sheets on top, then minced meat and lasagna sheets again in alternation
    (in my form the mass is enough for four layers).
    Finish with ground meat mixture, season again with salt and pepper,
    then spread the cup of sour cream on top. Finally, spread the package of cheese on top.

  3. The whole comes then with me for approx. 40 min. in the oven preheated to 175Â°C.
