Ingredients

  • 300 g sugar snap peas
  • 250 g chorizo sausage
  • 4 tablespoons almonds (skin)
  • 1 small bunch of thyme
  • salt, pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 packets (250 g each) pappardelle (refrigerated)
  • 150 ml dry white wine

Steps

  1. Clean, wash and halve the sugar snap peas. Cut the chorizo into slices.
    Coarsely chop the almonds. Wash the thyme, shake dry and pluck the leaves.
    Boil 2 l salted water (1 tsp salt per liter).

  2. Roast almonds and sausage in a frying pan without fat, then remove.
    Heat butter in the frying fat. Add the thyme leaves. Fry eggs in it to make fried eggs.

  3. Cook pasta and sugar snap peas in boiling salted water for about 2 minutes. Remove fried eggs from pan.
    Deglaze pan with wine, bring to a boil, season with salt if necessary. Add sausage and almonds.

  4. Drain pasta and sugar snap peas and return to pot. Mix in chorizo-almond sauce, season with pepper.
    Serve pasta with fried eggs. Serve with grated Manchego.
