Steps

  1. Fry the onions until hot and add the minced meat and garlic. Let everything fry for about 5 minutes.
    Deglaze with the pizza tomatoes and the strained tomatoes.
    Season with herbs, sugar, pepper, salt and a little chili - if you like. For consistency,
    add the tomato paste, a whole tube if needed.

  2. Now fill the minced meat mixture thinly into a preferably square baking dish, so that the lasagne plates fit.
    Put a layer of lasagna sheets on top, then minced meat and lasagna sheets again in alternation
    (in my form the mass is enough for four layers). Finish with ground meat mixture,
    season again with salt and pepper, then spread the cup of sour cream on top.
    Finally, spread the package of cheese on top.
